Hi, my name is

Zack Fischmann

I'm an artist, an adventurer, and I like to get things done

When I was 19 years old, I hitch-hiked from California to Maine in seven days. Six months later, I was supporting myself by busking on streetcorners and playing music in bars and coffeeshops. I eventually formed and joined several bands, founded a record label, toured across America, performed hundreds of shows for thousands of people, and produced several albums, one of which was released on my favorite record label ever.

After living on the road for several years, I accepted a full scholarship to study at the University of California, Berkeley, where I designed my own program called Change and Innovation. At Cal, I completed the Center for Entrepreneurship and Technology Concentration in Engineering Leadership, won first place at the Big Ideas @ Berkeley competition, won Fastest Growing Startup at the European Innovation Academy in Tallinn, Estonia, and founded and served as Co-President of the Center for Entrepreneurship and Technology Student Association. I also maintained a 4.0 GPA and graduated with Highest Honors.

I'm currently learning web development and experimenting with a project I hope to release in the near future. You can get a sense of what it will be from this blog. You can find me writing code at hackathons in the S.F. Bay Area, drinking beer with friends in Oakland, or playing banjo in my underwear at home.
