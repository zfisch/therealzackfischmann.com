When I was 19 years old, I ventured off on the most insane and awesome experience of my life. I also founded and currently lead U.C. Berkeley's strongest technology entrepreneurship club.

I have accomplished all of this while maintaining a 4.0 GPA, and teaching myself web development in my free time.

I will be graduating in May of 2014, and I'm looking for the next adventure!
